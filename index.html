CYBER TIPS

Stay safe in the digital world with these valuable CyberTips from Barbie:

  • Always use strong and unique passwords for your online accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
  • Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments in emails.
  • Regularly update your operating system and software for security patches.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information online, especially on public networks.
